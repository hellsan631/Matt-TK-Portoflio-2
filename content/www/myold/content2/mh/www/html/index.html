A Modded Minecraft RPG* Experience

*RPG. abbr. [är-pē-jē] : meaning role-playing game. A game in which players assume the roles of characters in a fictional setting. Players take responsibility for acting out these roles within a narrative, either through literal acting, or through a process of structured decision-making or character development.

Popular instances include the board game, Dungeons & Dungeons; the video game series, Final Fantasy; or the online social-adventure game, World of Warcraft.

Client-Side
modifications

Many minecraft servers may have one, or several mods that provide more content, in one or two areas. Often, developers of these mods, who have similar goals, create a linked compatability between each modification.

By making use of several linked mods on the client, we are able to provide a more open-ended world of creation. Each mod serves a purpose, and is configured to minimize the impact on the overall economy of the game.

Current Client-side Modifications include:

  • IndustrialCraft² (IC²) - v 1.95b
  • Equivalent Exchange 2 (EE) - v 1.4.5.1
  • BuildCraft (BC) - v 2.2.14
  • RedPower 2 - v 2.0p5b2
  • Forestry - v 1.4.6.1
  • RailCraft - v 5.2.4
  • (Planned) PowerCraft

Included in the package is a few performance and inferface addons that enhance the overall minecraft experience.

We strive for a bug-free enviroment. We are constantly updating and upgrading to the latest stable builds, but certain things may be disabled that are found to be buggy.

We Use
Bukkit

Notch said, let there be creatvity. And it was fun. But then the mods started flowing in. The Community said, let there be Forge. Then all was good, for a while. Servers grew, and administration was needed. The community said, let there be bukkit. And all was not well. Bukkit didn't like forge. For a time, war. Then, The community said, let there be Minecraft Port Central.

Our bukkit modifications are many. Server side plugin's aim at enhancing gameplay goals and at adding mechanics to the game of minecraft. While our client side adds content, our server gives you a better playground for that content.
